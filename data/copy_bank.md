| ----------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Validated   | We actually validate AI systems    |

## Words We Avoid

| Word/Phrase            | Why                          |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Digital transformation | Buzzword, means nothing      |
| Synergies              | Corporate speak              |
| Cutting-edge           | Overused, unverifiable       |
| I think maybe          | Hedging undermines authority |
| Revolutionary          | Hyperbolic                   |
