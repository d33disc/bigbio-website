## Words We Use

| Word/Phrase | Why                                |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Validated   | We actually validate AI systems    |
| Regulated   | Our domain is regulated industries |
| Shipped     | We deliver, not just advise        |
| Operator    | Practitioner, not just advisor     |
| Verified    | Claims backed by receipts          |

## Words We Avoid

| Word/Phrase            | Why                          |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Digital transformation | Buzzword, means nothing      |
| Synergies              | Corporate speak              |
| Cutting-edge           | Overused, unverifiable       |
| I think maybe          | Hedging undermines authority |
| Revolutionary          | Hyperbolic                   |

---

## Tone & Voice Notes

- Direct, confident, no hedging
- Metric-driven -- back claims with numbers
- Practitioner voice, not consultant voice (we build, not just advise)
- First person for CTA sections, third person for About
- Navy + emerald color palette reinforces authority + action
